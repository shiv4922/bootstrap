Welcome Gwalior

Gwalior , India 's Madhya Pradesh state is a major city. From geographical point of view Gwalior M.P. Located in the north of the state. This city and its famous fort are the centers of ancient cities of northern India. The great warriors, kings, poets, musicians and saints contributed to the recognition of this capital as a nationwide identity.

After this, in the coming centuries, this city is the Rajasthani of the largest dynasties. With every century, the history of this city will get new dimensions.
